About Our Founder

Speaker | Mentor | International Internship | Consultant | Business Developer | Product Manager

Rakesh Raushan, Founder & CEO of TechTable Pursuing an Integrated Degree in Aerospace Engineering from IIT Madras. His keen interest in Entrepreneurship and passion towards solving problems of society has enabled him to start TechTable which enables students to get Hands on experience by working on Live projects. This has skilled thousands of Engineers and guided many to choose and pursue different career options.

His Experience varied from being a consultant for startups and Handling projects too. He has mentored 100+ students directly to boost their careers. He has worked with Big Companies and startups like Lotte India and EaseMyTrip.com and gave a tremendous output. His experience varies from Counselling and guiding students to Digital Marketing and Business Development.
